WHO AM I?

Hi, I'm Somanath Giri, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Information Technology. As a passionate technology enthusiast and problem-solver, I am always driven to build practical and impactful digital solutions that solve real-world challenges.

Found-Hub was created after observing a common campus issue — the stress and inconvenience caused by lost items such as wallets, ID cards, gadgets, and important belongings. I envisioned a smarter, centralized digital platform where students and staff could easily report, search, and recover lost items in a secure and structured way.

This platform represents more than just a web application. It reflects my interest in full-stack development, system design, and building scalable solutions. By combining modern web technologies with intelligent features, Found-Hub aims to transform the traditional lost-and-found process into a seamless, transparent, and technology-driven experience.

My goal is to continuously innovate and develop solutions that enhance campus life, improve efficiency, and promote a culture of responsibility and trust within the student community.
